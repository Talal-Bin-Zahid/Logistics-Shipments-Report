## Executive Summary

This Power BI Logistics Shipments Analysis Dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of shipment performance across destinations and carriers. The report highlights a strong delivery success rate of **82.26%**, indicating efficient logistics operations, while also uncovering cost, distance, and carrier-level performance patterns to support data-driven decision-making.

## Key Insights

* A high delivery rate of over 82% reflects strong overall operational efficiency.
* Carriers with higher average distances, such as FedEx, tend to have higher average shipment costs.
* USPS demonstrates cost efficiency, making it suitable for shorter-distance or cost-sensitive shipments.
* Comparing cost and distance together helps identify optimal carrier selection strategies.
